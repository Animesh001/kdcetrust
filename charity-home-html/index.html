OUR MISSION

To promote and impart knowledge, promote moral and spiritual values, inspire creativity and establish social awareness.

To initiate, establish and maintain a college/institution for imparting education, conducting research, planning, evaluating, developing and enhancing knowledge and understanding in the field of health care and pharmacy industries

To create and enhance opportunities in the health care industries in general and the State of West Bengal in particular.

To train and equip the public to become self-reliant in a dignified and civilized lifestyle so as to develop into good, healthy and progressive citizens.

To provide training on job oriented education well recognized by the respective industries in India and abroad.

OUR VISION

The Institute has a clear vision and objective in its quest to be a center of excellence for learning and development of human resources, to adapt to the rapidly changing national and international conditions. It will follow a coherent recruitment policy to recruit the very best teachers specializing in various fields of pharmacy and healthcare sector. Apart from meeting the needs of higher education in Siliguri, the institute will also aim to fulfill the dreams of students coming from states like Sikkim, Nepal Bangladesh and Bihar and 7 North Eastern states. The institute is committed to develop itself as a value and need based quality education provider in India and especially in the state of West Bengal. Its ultimate goal is to produce qualified and capable manpower for the changing needs of the society at the national and international level.
